Differentiated in finetuning (more commodified in pretraining)

Bridgewater (w/ Thinking Machines) fine-tuned Qwen3-235B via Tinker (GRPO + CISPO + on-policy distillation) on expert-labeled financial judgment tasks — beat frontier models (Opus, Gemini, GPT) on accuracy at ~1/14th the inference cost. Concrete case for the differentiated-finetuning thesis: proprietary data + finetuning > raw frontier scale for tacit expert judgment. [[finance]]
