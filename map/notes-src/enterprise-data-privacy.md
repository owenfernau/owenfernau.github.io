---
title: Enterprise Data Privacy
---

Anthropic/OpenAI etc. are in effect learning the workflows of the companies that use them — every enterprise prompt is a window into how that business actually operates.

Value opportunity: guaranteeing prompt/data protection. "Important enterprises" won't hand over IP to a model provider if there's a risk it gets sold back to them or resold on the broader market.

Where does value accrue in enterprise AI adoption? Whoever can credibly promise data isolation captures the trust — and the deal. [[finance]]
