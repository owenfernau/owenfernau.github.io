Routing AI safety's people, not its content

Concept mockup. Not a live product. Built to test one idea: matching on what you're specifically working on or stuck on, not on a broad interest tag.


There are more people in AI safety who should be talking to each other than currently are. Proximity (the same Slack, the same EAG, the same Discord) doesn't produce connection, because nothing in those tools matches on the specific thing you're working on, only the broad category. This page mocks up what a specific, justified intro looks like, and what the intake profile behind it would ask.

Example matches


Stuck on: building a clean activation-patching pipeline for interpretability work → paired with someone who already built and shipped one
